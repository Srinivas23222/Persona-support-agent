Password Reset Guide

Overview
This guide helps users reset their account password securely.

Steps to Reset Password

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click on "Forgot Password".
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Click the "Send Reset Link" button.
5. Check your email inbox for the password reset link.
6. Click the link and create a new password.
7. Log in using the new password.

Common Issues

Issue: Reset email not received
Solution:
- Check the spam or junk folder.
- Verify that the email address is correct.
- Wait up to 10 minutes for email delivery.

Issue: Reset link expired
Solution:
- Request a new password reset link.
- Use the most recent email received.

Issue: Account remains locked
Solution:
- Wait 30 minutes and try again.
- Contact customer support if the issue persists.

Security Recommendations

- Use a strong password with at least 8 characters.
- Include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
- Do not share passwords with anyone.

Contact Support

If password reset continues to fail after following all steps, contact customer support for further assistance.
